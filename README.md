This mod is designed to be lightweight and modular, and to work well with any other mod. 
It includes a built in price display to support accurate equipment value, loot value, and end of raid calculations, so everything works out of the box without requiring other mods.
If you prefer another pricing mod or simply don’t want the built in price display, you can disable it in the F12 config.
You can choose which price source to use, trader prices or flea prices, and almost everything can be enabled, disabled, or adjusted depending on how you want the mod to behave.
The goal of this mod is not to replace existing pricing mods, but to provide accurate and reliable equipment and loot value calculations during and outside of raids.
Features
Shows item values on hover
Show value of loot in backpacks, rigs and cases
Shows total equipment value outside raids
Tracks loot value during raids
Displays total loot value at the end of a raid
Notes
Price accuracy is based on the selected source, trader or flea
Configuration (F12 menu)
Enable or disable tooltip prices
Switch between trader and flea price source
Toggle breakdowns such as ammo, contents, plates, and attachments
Choose between compact or more detailed price display
Compatibility
Built to work with most mods, but compatibility can vary with mods that replace tooltip logic or heavily change trader or flea pricing
